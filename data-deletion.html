Data Deletion

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Deleting your account and data

In the app (fastest): If you're signed in, go to Profile → Delete account, confirm, and your account and all associated personal data are deleted immediately and permanently.

By email: You can also request deletion at any time by emailing support@wedgietrivia.com from the email address on your account (or include your account email and display name) with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will permanently delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days and confirm by email once it is complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials (email, display name).
  • Profile information you provided (such as favorite color, food, movie, music, hobby, birthday, hometown, fun fact, and dream vacation).
  • Gameplay data tied to your account, such as match history.

Information that we are legally required to retain may be kept as required by applicable law, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Guest accounts

If you played as a guest, your data is stored locally on your device. You can remove it by clearing your browser's site data for play.wedgietrivia.com, or by contacting us at the address above.

Sign in with Facebook, Google, or Apple

If you signed in using Facebook, Google, or Apple, deleting your Wedgie Trivia account removes the data we hold. It does not change your account with that provider — you can also revoke Wedgie Trivia's access from your Facebook, Google, or Apple account settings.

Contact

For any questions about deleting your data, contact us at support@wedgietrivia.com.
